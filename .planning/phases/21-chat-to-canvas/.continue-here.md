## Critical Anti-Patterns (do NOT repeat these)

- ANTI-PATTERN: Adding caps "to be safe" without checking the model's actual window. Qwen3.6 Plus is 991K/64K; old caps were sized for a tiny window and wasted ~99% of available context. → STRUCTURAL: when raising/lowering a numeric cap, cite the model spec or it doesn't get changed.
- ANTI-PATTERN: Designing a heuristic as an allow-list of keywords. Misses everything you didn't think of. → STRUCTURAL: prefer default-on with a small negation list when the goal is generality.
- ANTI-PATTERN: Telling the LLM about a tool without specifying the parameter shape. The model fired create_plan with only a title because the schema's `content` field was undocumented. → STRUCTURAL: every tool parameter that the model is supposed to fill MUST have its concrete shape (with example) in the description.
